## 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains GIS mapping projects completed during public health training at the Florida Department of Health – Miami-Dade County.

The maps were developed using ArcGIS Pro to support spatial visualization, geographic analysis, and public health communication.

---

## 🗺 Featured Maps

### ZIP Code Mapping

Developed a geographic visualization of ZIP code boundaries to support spatial understanding of community-level public health data.

**Skills Demonstrated**
- ArcGIS Pro
- Geographic Boundary Mapping
- Layer Management
- Cartographic Design
- Spatial Data Visualization

---

### High-Density Population Mapping

Created a thematic map highlighting high-density geographic areas to support population-based public health planning and resource allocation.

**Skills Demonstrated**
- Spatial Analysis
- Thematic Mapping
- Population Visualization
- GIS-Based Decision Support
- Public Health Planning

---

## 💡 Public Health Relevance

GIS mapping is an important tool in public health for:

- Disease surveillance
- Resource allocation
- Community health assessment
- Emergency preparedness
- Population health planning

These projects demonstrate foundational GIS skills used in epidemiology and public health practice.
